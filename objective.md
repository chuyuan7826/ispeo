# English version
Dry Lab (Computational) Track:
Suppose you want to design a model to describe the expression/production of a product in a metabolic pathway that has been introduced into E. coli (e.g., squalene, vinblastine—please specify). How would you design the plan?
1. To describe the relationship between cell growth and product formation, what type of model would you choose? For example: ordinary differential equations (ODEs)? metabolic flux analysis? Briefly explain why.
2. Write the core equations of your model, define key parameters, and explain the biological meaning of those parameters.
3. Use AI to generate some hypothetical experimental data. Substitute the data into your model for analysis—what conclusions can you draw? How would you optimize the model? How would you optimize the experiments? What is the expected outcome after optimization?

# 中文版
干实验方向：
假设你准备设计一个模型，描述已嵌入大肠杆菌中的某个代谢通路中某个产物的表达（如：角鲨烯、长春花碱，请注明），你将如何设计实验方案？
1. 为了描述细胞生长与产物之间的关系，你想选择哪种模型？比如常微分方程？代谢流分析模型？简述理由。
2. 写出你模型中的核心方程，定义关键参数并阐明参数的生物学意义。
3. 利用 AI 生成一些假想的实验数据，将数据代入你的模型中进行分析，你能得到什么结论？如何进行模型优化？如何进行实验优化？优化后的预期是什么？
